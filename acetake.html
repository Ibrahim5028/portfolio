About Acetake Group

The Acetake Group, based in Canada, is a distinguished engineering design and consulting firm specializing in the industrial and manufacturing sectors. They are acclaimed for their custom-engineered solutions, focusing on the design and manufacture of high-quality precision tools. Additionally, they assist clients in acquiring various licenses required for production activities.

My Time at Acetake Group


*Due to an NDA, I can only disclose limited information regarding my work at Acetake Group


In my 4-month internship at Acetake Group, I held the position of Mechanical Engineering Intern within the company.


I took the lead on enhancing high-volume manufacturing methods, including injection molding, die casting, stamping, and extrusion. By implementing lean manufacturing principles, I was able to increase production efficiency by 15% and decrease material waste by 20%. This effort involved a careful analysis of existing processes and the introduction of streamlined practices.


Utilizing Ansys CFD models, I conducted fluid flow simulations under high wind conditions. This advanced modeling enabled me to optimize CAD designs, ensuring they met target parameters for performance and safety. Additionally, I performed FEA analysis on these CAD models to assess the impacts of structural, thermal, and dynamic forces, identifying critical areas for improvement and potential limitations in design.


Furthermore, I guided 13 clients through the TSSA certification process with minimal supervision. My comprehensive project management skills and attention to detail led to a 100% success rate in obtaining certifications, significantly enhancing operational compliance and safety standards for these clients.
