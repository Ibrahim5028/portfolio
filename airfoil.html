Airfoil Simulation Project

About the Project


Driven by my fascination with aviation and utilizing my expertise in fluid dynamics, I was aiming to improve the airfoil design of my RC plane using CFD. Initially, the airfoil had sharp edges that created excessive drag and turbulence.


By refining the design to be smoother and more aerodynamic, I significantly enhanced its performance. The revised airfoil design on the RC plane not only generated more lift but also flew flawlessly, demonstrating a successful optimization of its design.


Skill Acquired:


Aerodynamic Optimization, Design Refinement, Performance Analysis, Practical Problem-Solving, CFD Analysis
