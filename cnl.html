About Canadian Nuclear Labratories

Canadian Nuclear Laboratories (CNL), located in Chalk River, Ontario, specializes in nuclear science, focusing on medicine, energy solutions, and global nuclear challenges. CNL is well known for helping develop the CANDU nuclear reactor technology and is currently working on small modular reactors (SMRs). Committed to safety and environmental stewardship, CNL is a key influencer in the international nuclear sector.
