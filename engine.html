Engine Project

About the Project


As the leader of the Fuel and Air Division at Lassonde Motorsports club, I managed a team focused on optimizing a Suzuki 4 Cylinder 800cc F8a Carburetor Engine. We enhanced the engine by improving the compression ratio, transitioning to an electronic ignition system, and upgrading the intake and exhaust systems. The introduction of a turbocharger/supercharger and the rebuilding of components using CAD and machining tools, alongside ECU tuning, resulted in a more efficient and powerful engine.


We strictly adhered to Formula SAE rules and ASME Y14.5 GD&T standards, ensuring our modifications met all specifications. Through CFD analysis with Star-CCM+ on the air intake system, we ensured optimal air supply to the combustion chamber and refined the design based on these insights. Root cause analysis was conducted to address combustion instability, identifying insufficient oxygen injection as the key issue, leading to targeted improvements.


Skill Acquired:


Team Management, Mechanical Modification, Airflow Optimization, CAD and Machining, ECU Tuning, Performance Analysis
