RC Plane Project

About the Project


As a mechanical engineering student with a deep passion for airplanes, I undertook a project to build a custom remote-controlled plane. This endeavor allowed me to explore and understand aerodynamics and aircraft control systems in a hands-on manner.


Building this plane from scratch gave me the opportunity to apply the theoretical knowledge I've gained in my studies, particularly in fluid dynamics and mechanical design. It was a practical way to delve into the complexities of aircraft behavior and control, enhancing my skills and deepening my understanding of aviation technology.


This project was not just an academic exercise; it was a step towards fulfilling my long-standing fascination with flight and flight mechanics.


Skill Acquired:


Aerodynamics Understanding, Mechanical Design, Hands-On Assembly, Control Systems Insight, Problem-Solving and Innovation
