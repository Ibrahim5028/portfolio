About Pratt & Whitney

Pratt & Whitney Canada a subsidiary of United Technologies Corporation and located in Longueil, Quebec specializes in Jet engine design & manufacturing. Pratt & Whitney is noted for developing engines for the F22 Raptor fighter jet and SR-71 Blackbird reconnaissance aircraft. They are also known for developing engines for civilian aircraft, business jets, and helicopter. Their work emphasizes engine efficiency and environmental sustainability, significantly advancing aerospace technology.

My Time at Pratt & Whitney

In my 8-month internship at Pratt & Whitney, I held the position of Engineering Supply Chain Analyst Intern within the Supplier Quality Assurance Group. This group is primarily tasked with ensuring compliance of supplier parts with both TCCA and corporate standards. A key aspect of our work involved the thorough inspection of parts, guaranteeing their quality and adherence to required specifications before advancing to subsequent stages of inspection.


At Pratt & Whitney, my internship as an Engineering Supply Chain Analyst Intern involved rigorous inspection of 165 supplier supply chain records, I ensured 100% compliance with TCCA and Company specifications were achieved.


Collaborating with suppliers, I successfully resolved supply chain reporting issues, enhancing compliance by 10%. I also developed a more efficient supply chain record form, simplifying the reporting process and improving efficiency by 20%.
