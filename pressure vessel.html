Pressure Vessel Project

About the Project


During my internship at Acetake, one of our industrial sector clients requested the creation of a mechanical design and a 3D CAD model for a pressure vessel needed in their industrial process. Utilizing the specifications provided by the client, I successfully designed and developed a pressure vessel that met their specific needs and requirements.


The design of the pressure vessel included detailed Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing (GD&T) and welding symbols. Prototyping played a crucial role due to several design modifications and enhancements, which were implemented based on feedback from the client and my supervisor.


Skill Acquired:


CAD Modeling, Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing (GD&T), Understanding Welding Symbols, Client Collaboration, Prototype Development
