About the Project


As a member of my university’s robotics team, my role involved creating CAD designs for a robotic arm, tailored to meet specific requirements of a design competition we planned to enter. This process entailed multiple design iterations and prototypes for refinement.


Additionally, I was responsible for machining key components from a block of aluminum using CNC techniques, showcasing my practical skills in both design and manufacturing.


Skill Acquired:


CAD Design, Iterative Design Process, CNC Machining, Team Collaboration, Problem-Solving
