Steel Beams Project

About the Project


During my internship at Acetake, one of our industrial sector clients requested the creation of a mechanical design and a 3D CAD model for steel beams needed in their building renovation project.


Utilizing the specifications provided by the client, I successfully determined the most optimal steel beams configuration and placement that met their specific needs and requirements. The design of the steel beams included detailed Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing (GD&T), welding symbols, and a bill of materials for the recommended bolts.


Skill Acquired:


Structural Design, CAD Modeling, Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing (GD&T), Understanding Welding Symbols, Bill of Materials Creation
