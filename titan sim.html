Titan Simulation Project

About the Project


CFD was used to provide a comprehensive analysis of hydrodynamic resistance encountered by the Titan submersible during various maneuvers, including movements in different directions. This CFD modeling was essential to understand the submersible's hydrodynamic behavior under different steering scenarios.


By doing so, I was able to help identify areas for potential performance enhancements, which were crucial for the submersible's safety and functionality under deep-sea conditions. This analysis helped ensure that the Titan's design was suited for the extreme environments it was meant to operate in.


Skill Acquired:


CFD Analysis, Hydrodynamic Behavior Understanding, Performance Optimization, Safety and Design Suitability Analysis
